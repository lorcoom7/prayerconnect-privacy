PrayerConnect — Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2025

Contact Email: sh31don@duck.com

1. Introduction

PrayerConnect is a mobile app designed to help users deepen their prayer life, fasting discipline, worship time, and spiritual well-being. Your privacy is extremely important to us. This policy explains what information we collect and how it is used.

2. Information We Collect

  • Prayer requests you type or speak — stored only on your device unless you choose to share them.
  • Fasting selections and progress — stored locally on your device.
  • Worship playlists — links to public YouTube content; no tracking occurs.
  • App usage data — anonymous analytics may be collected to improve app performance.

3. How We Use Information

  • To enable app features such as prayer, fasting timers, and worship playlists.
  • To improve app reliability, navigation, and performance.
  • To help understand which features users engage with, without identifying you.

4. What We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal identity information
  • No contact list access
  • No location tracking
  • No financial or payment information
  • No biometric data
  • No data from your device outside the app

5. Data Storage

Your prayer requests, fasting logs, and settings are stored locally on your device only. We do not operate a cloud database for user content.

6. Third-Party Services

The app may use trusted services such as YouTube for worship videos. These third-party services have their own privacy policies.

7. Children’s Privacy

PrayerConnect is safe for all ages and does not collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated. Changes will be published on this page.

9. Contact

If you have any questions, email us at sh31don@duck.com.
